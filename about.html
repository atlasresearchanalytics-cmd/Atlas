About Atlas

Rigor is our starting point. Insight is the goal.

We're a research and data consultancy that exists to make good research more achievable — for the people doing it under real deadlines, real budgets and real academic standards.

Who We Are

A consultancy built around the full research lifecycle.

Atlas Research Analytics supports students, academics, researchers and institutions across every stage of a research project — research design and proposal development, data collection, statistical and qualitative analysis, interpretation, visualization and academic reporting. Rather than handling pieces in isolation, we work as a single point of support from your first research question through to your final write-up, so nothing gets lost in the handoff between stages.

Mission

Empowering rigorous research.

To equip students, academics, researchers and institutions with reliable, methodologically sound research and data analysis support — so good ideas are never let down by weak execution.

Vision

A trusted name in research analytics.

To become a leading research and academic analytics consultancy — known for precision, integrity and genuinely useful support at every stage of the research process.

Our Values

What guides how we work.

Excellence

We hold our own work to the same standard your examiners or reviewers will.

Integrity

Honest analysis and honest advice — including when the data doesn't say what you hoped.

Collaboration

We work with you, not just for you, so you understand your own results.

Empowerment

Our goal is to build your confidence with your data, not dependence on us.

Innovation

We stay current with methods and tools so your work does too.

Continuous Learning

Research standards evolve — so does our practice.

Our Team

Researchers, statisticians and academic writers.

Atlas is run by a small team with backgrounds spanning research methodology, statistics and academic writing, working together so your project gets both technical accuracy and a write-up that reads well. Individual team profiles are on the way — in the meantime, every project starts with a direct conversation with the person who'll actually be working on it.
